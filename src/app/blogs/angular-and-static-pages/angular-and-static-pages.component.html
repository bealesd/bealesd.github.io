Generating Static Pages in Angular with Scully

Overview

Static websites are great, here are some of the reason why:

  • simple to setup
  • easy to host
  • fast load times
  • more secure
For a simple site, like my blog, static sites are ideal. However, I've written my blog in Angular, which is dynamic. Welcome scullyio. Scully will take yor Angular website, find all your routes, and generates a static website.
